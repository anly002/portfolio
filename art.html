Inspired by Apple's 'liquid glass' aesthetic, I explored a refined glassmorphism approach that balances clarity and performance. Because the illusion relies on contrast and depth, I designed a simple, color-shifting background that brings the translucent surfaces to life.

To showcase the drawings effectively, I implemented interactive image sliders that let viewers explore each piece.
